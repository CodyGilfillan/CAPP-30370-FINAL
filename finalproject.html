Mission

Smooth Move Smoothies is dedicated to bringing a healthy, late-night snack to students at Notre Dame by deliverying smoothies to your dorm on weekday nights.

Why Smooth Moves?

Imagine you just had a healthy day of eating and spent 30 minutes on the treadmill at Rolf's. Don't go to Taco Bell, Pizza Hut, or the Huddle for a quarter dog! Order a healthy smoothie to supplement your healthy day of eating!

The Smooth Move Difference

To assure every customer received the freshest smoothie, our fruit is bought THE SAME DAY you order your smoothie.
